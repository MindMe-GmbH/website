What MindMe! is

Rigorous science, on a screen an animal actually wants to use.

MindMe! is a touchscreen-based cognitive enrichment and behavioural platform.

Built for the home and the lab alike, the device pairs a waterproof touchscreen with an integrated feeder and adaptive software.

Inside the device

Cognitive games

Self-paced tasks grounded in over a decade of cognitive and behavioural research.

Performance tracking

Every session is measured, so progress and well-being are visible over time.

Self-paced interaction

Animals engage voluntarily, at their own rhythm — no handler required.
